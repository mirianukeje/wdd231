About Us

At Cards From My Heart, we believe that the simplest gestures can leave the deepest impressions. A card isn't just paper and ink, it's a piece of your heart, a way to say I care, I'm thinking of you, or I'm proud of you without even having to be in the same room. Founded in 2023 from a deep love for design and meaningful connection, our journey began with a simple mission: to help people express their feelings in the most beautiful and personal way possible. What started as a passion project has grown into a space where creativity meets emotion, offering both ready-made designs for life's quick moments and custom-made creations for occasions that deserve something truly unique. From birthdays and weddings to “just because” surprises, every card and gift we create is crafted with attention to detail, love for artistry, and an understanding that your message matters. We're here to make sure your words are seen, felt, and remembered, one card at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cards From My Heart?

Cards From My Heart is a creative platform offering unique meme cards for every occasion. We help you share laughter, love, and memorable moments with custom card designs.

How do I order a custom card?

You can order a custom card by visiting our Contact Us page and submitting your request. We'll work with you to create a card that fits your occasion perfectly.

Can I personalize the message on my card?

Yes! All our cards can be personalized with your own message, names, or special dates. Just let us know your preferences when you order.

What occasions do you cover?

We create cards for birthdays, celebrations, anniversaries, holidays, and “just because” moments. If you have a unique occasion, contact us and we'll design something special.

How are cards delivered?

Cards are delivered digitally via email. Physical delivery may be available on request—please ask us on the Contact Us page.

Can I request a bulk order?

Yes, we accept bulk orders for events, organizations, or businesses. Reach out through our Contact Us page for details and pricing.

How do I get in touch for support or questions?

For any questions or support, please use our Contact Us page and we'll respond promptly.
