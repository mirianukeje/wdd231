Mirian Ukeje - Website Plan

Site Name: Cards From My Heart

The name Cards From My Heart was chosen because it reflects the emotional and personal touch behind every card created. Each design comes from a place of sincerity, creativity, and care not just something printed, but something that carries meaning.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Cards From My Heart website is to showcase and promote a small creative business that specializes in designing heartfelt, custom greeting cards for various occasions. The site will serve as a portfolio to display unique card collections, share the story and inspiration behind the brand, and allow customers to easily browse designs, request custom orders, and get in touch. By building an online presence, the website aims to help the business reach a wider audience, create emotional connections through design, and make it easier for people to express their feelings through beautifully crafted cards.

Scenarios

Here is what our visitors are asking and how Cards From My Heart makes it easy to find answers!

  • Can I order a card with my own message and design?
  • Where can I see examples of your cards before ordering?
  • How long does it take to receive a custom card?
  • Do you offer digital versions or only printed cards?
